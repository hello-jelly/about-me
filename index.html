Meet Bisbee!


A little about me...

I'm Bisbee, a 7 year old Exotic Shorthair cat. I'm just a little thing, weighing in at 6 pounds. I was passed around 3 homes before getting rescued by my current owners in 2013. I live in SE Portland with my family of 9. My favorite people are my Mom and Dad, and I enjoy the birds as well: Ringo, Rosie and Siggy. I tolerate the dogs: Suede, Nimbus and Desmond, but I absolutely detest the newest member, Jerome...the other cat.

My hobbies include napping on the wine rack, sitting in the fireplace, peering at you from behind a wall, knocking any and all objects off surfaces, and cramming myself into the littlest boxes. I also love to leave little tufts of hair all around the house. It's great exercise for my dad, having to pick them up behind me.

Bisbee loves, in order...

  1. Getting all the attention
  2. When Mom puts bonito flakes on her kibble
  3. Feather teasers and yarn
  4. Long hours staring out the window
  5. A squeaky clean litter box

Bisbee hates...

  • When Jerome chases her
  • When Jerome steals her food
  • When Jerome intrudes on her playtime
  • ...Jerome
