Meet Bisbee!


Hello Everyone...

I'm Bisbee, a 7 year old Exotic Shorthair cat. I was passed around 3 homes before
getting rescued by my current owners in 2013. I live in SE Portland with my family
of 9. My favorite people are my Mom and Dad, and I enjoy the birds as well: Ringo,
Rosie and Siggy. I tolerate the dogs: Suede, Nimbus and Desmond, but I absolutely
detest the newest member, Jerome...the other cat. My hobbies include napping on
the wine rack, sitting in the fireplace, peering at you from behind a wall, knocking all
objects off all surfaces, and cramming myself into tiny boxes.

Bisbee loves, in order...

  1. getting all the attention
  2. when Mom puts bonito flakes on her kibble
  3. feather teasers and yarn
  4. long hours staring out the window
  5. a sparkling clean litter box

Bisbee hates...

  • when Jerome chases her
  • when Jerome steals her food
  • when Jerome intrudes on her playtime
  • ...Jerome
