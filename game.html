About Me Guess Game Kai-Bai-Bo!

Kai Bai Bo is a variation of the westernized game "Rock Scissors Paper".
Ka-wi (가위) is Scissors, Ba-wi (바위) is Rock, and Bo (보) is Cloth.
The rules of the game are the same, and the result is final.
It's the great equalizer in Korean society, a time-honored method of
making any and all decisions, and the greatest tiebreaker known to man.
